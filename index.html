Who we are

We don't advise on infrastructure. We build it.

First Tactical Projects is a Scottish infrastructure developer built around a leadership team with over 60 years of combined experience delivering complex energy and capital projects globally. We work with global capital partners and investment grade EPC vendors to take sites from concept to construction — with the investment relationships and development discipline to see it through.
